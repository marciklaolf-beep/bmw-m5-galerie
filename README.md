# BMW F90 M5 Documentation

Welcome to the BMW F90 M5 website documentation. This documentation provides an overview of the features, specifications, and maintenance of the BMW F90 M5.

## Overview
The BMW F90 M5 is a high-performance sports sedan produced by BMW M GmbH. It is known for its powerful engine, luxurious interiors, and advanced technology.

## Features
- **Engine**: 4.4-liter BMW M TwinPower Turbo V8
- **Power**: 600 hp (441 kW) and 553 lb-ft (748 Nm) of torque
- **Performance**: 0-60 mph in just 3.2 seconds
- **Transmission**: 8-speed M Steptronic automatic transmission
- **Drivetrain**: BMW xDrive all-wheel-drive system

## Specifications
- **Length**: 4940 mm
- **Width**: 1940 mm
- **Height**: 1466 mm
- **Wheelbase**: 2982 mm
- **Curb Weight**: 1855 kg

## Maintenance
Regular maintenance is key to keeping your BMW F90 M5 in optimal condition. Follow the recommended service intervals as specified in the owner's manual.

For any issues, do not hesitate to contact your local BMW dealership or service center.
